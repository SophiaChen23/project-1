Services Page

Child Swimming Lessons

Our child swimming lessons are tailored for children aged 3-10 and are designed to build water confidence and develop swimming skills in a safe and fun environment.

  • Small class sizes to ensure individual attention
  • Warm water pools for comfort
  • Experienced and certified instructors
  • Structured lessons focusing on water safety and basic swimming techniques
  • Age-appropriate activities to engage and motivate children
  • Progressive learning approach to build skills and confidence

Additional Services

In addition to child swimming lessons, we also offer the following services:

  • Swim Team Training
  • Private Swimming Lessons
  • Water Safety Workshops
  • Parent and Child Aquatics
  • Birthday Pool Parties

Contact us today to inquire about our services and get your child started on their swimming journey!
